Privacy Policy for BingeVault

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

BingeVault ("the App") is a personal media tracking application. While the core features work offline, we use third-party advertising services to support development.

2. Information Collection and Use

The App itself does not collect or store any personal information. All media tracking data (titles, progress, tags) is stored exclusively on your local device.

3. Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as:

  • IP Address
  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Device information and usage statistics

This data is used by Google to serve relevant ads and prevent fraud. You can view Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com.

4. Permissions

The App may request access to your device's photo gallery to select poster images. This data remains on your device. We also require Internet access to load advertisements.
